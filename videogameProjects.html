My video game projects.

A compilation of some video games that I have worked on during my education.

Gods vs Demons.

This game was a class project for which we had a very short deadline.

To Bee.

To Bee was one of my first works from university where we were still experimenting with video game engines like Unity and its tools.

Castellnovo Chronicles.

This is one of the most recent projects.
We are working on it with a team of 4 people, organized and strictly monitoring the development since it is an on-demand game.
