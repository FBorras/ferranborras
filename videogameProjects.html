My video game projects.

A compilation of some video games that I have worked on during my education.

Time Fight.

Time Fight won 3rd place in the Guerrilla Dev Contest.
A competition held in Madrid, Spain, in which 4 universities from the country participated.

La Hamburguesa Perfecta.

This is my last project done.
I've worked on it for a few weeks in a small team. This game tested our knowledge in pathfinding and AI behavior.

Toy-Survivor.

This is one of the most recent projects.
I've been working on it for a few months and it has a wide variety of optimization theoretical foundations applied.

Castellnovo Chronicles.

This is one of the biggest projects I faced.
I have worked on it with a team of 3 other people, organized and strictly following the development since it is an on-demand game.

Gods vs Demons.

This game was a class project for which we had a very short deadline.
The approach to the subject facilitated single-protagonist games, so carrying out this project was a challenge in which I had to investigate on my own.

ToBee.

ToBee was one of my first works from university where we were still experimenting with video game engines like Unity and its tools.
